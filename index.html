About Fixed Mark Digital

At Fixed Mark Digital, I do things a little differently. Yes, I build and polish websites — but my real focus is making sure you actually know how to use yours.

Too many business owners feel locked out of their own site, waiting days and paying fees for simple changes that shouldn't require a developer at all. I'm here to change that — through hands-on training, consult calls, custom video walkthroughs, and step-by-step documentation built around your specific site.

My goal is simple: the less you need me for the small stuff, the better I've done my job.
